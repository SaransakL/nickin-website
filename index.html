About Us

Nickin Game Studios (trading as Nickin) is a game development studio focused on creating experiences on Roblox, founded on October 31, 2025 by Saransak L. with a strong passion for game design, creativity, and community-driven development.

Nickin aims to build fun, polished, and memorable Roblox games with a strong focus on quality, atmosphere, and player experience, while continuing to grow, experiment, and refine ideas through every project we create.
